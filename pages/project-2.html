Personal Knowledge Base Book Library

Personal Project

This project, accessible at knowledge-base.ch, goes beyond a simple digital bookshelf to create a dynamic intellectual partner. It's an ecosystem where a curated collection of over 70 influential books—each with summaries, frameworks, and detailed chapter breakdowns—is transformed into an interactive knowledge graph.

AI-Enhanced Intellectual Partnership

The core innovation lies in the multi-layered AI integrations that allow you to overcome passive reading. Using advanced models from OpenAI and Gemini, you can synthesize ideas from different authors, generate deep cross-contextual insights, and apply complex theoretical frameworks to real-world use cases through a conversational interface. It's a tool that not only stores knowledge but enhances and extends your own thought process, turning a static library into a living, collaborative intelligence.

Key Features

  • Curated collection of over 70 influential books with detailed summaries and frameworks
  • Interactive knowledge graph connecting ideas across different authors and disciplines
  • Multi-layered AI integration with OpenAI and Gemini for deep insights
  • Cross-contextual synthesis enabling novel connections between concepts
  • Conversational interface for applying theoretical frameworks to real-world scenarios
  • Chapter-level breakdowns with actionable insights and key takeaways
